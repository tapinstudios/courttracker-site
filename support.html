Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add players to my team?

From the Home screen, tap on your team name to open the roster editor. Tap "Add Player" to enter a player's name and jersey number. You can also add a photo from your camera roll.

Can I track multiple teams?

Yes. From the Home screen you can create and manage multiple teams. Each team has its own roster, game history, and season stats.

How do substitutions work?

During a game, tap the SUBS button at the bottom of the screen. Select a player on the court to take out, then tap a bench player to put in. The clock can automatically pause during substitutions in Settings.

Can I export game stats?

Yes. After a game ends, tap the EXPORT button on the game summary screen. This generates a CSV file you can share via Messages, Email, AirDrop, or open in any spreadsheet app.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud or any server. If you delete the app, your data will be removed.

Does it work offline?

Yes, Court Tracker works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed.
