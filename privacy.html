Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

Overview

Court Tracker ("the App") is a basketball statistics tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Court Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter — including team rosters, player information, game statistics, and player photos — is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device identifiers.

No Network Access

The App does not connect to the internet. All functionality works entirely offline. No data is sent to any server.

Photos

If you choose to add player photos, images are selected from your device's photo library and stored locally within the App's private storage. Photos are never uploaded or shared.

Data Storage

All app data is stored on your device using standard iOS storage. If you delete the App, all associated data will be removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: tapinstudios@icloud.com
